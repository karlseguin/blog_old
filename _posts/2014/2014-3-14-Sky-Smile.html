Up amongst the sky,
I saw beauty for a mile.
Then she sat across the aisle
and my world became her smile.

It's nice to be reminded
of colours bold and vivid.
It's strange to feel alive,
with heart pounding. Hope, it thrives

There's laughter to my left,
this angel does not rest.
A chorus of such artistry.
A universal sobriety.
