Knowledge is the gift of experience,
pride its curse.
He who was a humble apprentice
abandons his thirst

What once we sought
now jealously guarded.
All others are not
and should be discarded

Knowledge is endless;
a blow to our pride.
Much easier to hide
behind imagined greatness

Experience, the paradox.
At first they flock
later they mock
